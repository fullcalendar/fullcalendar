
monthNames
==========

Full names of months.

<div class='spec' markdown='1'>
Array, *default*:

	['January', 'February', 'March', 'April', 'May', 'June', 'July',
	 'August', 'September', 'October', 'November', 'December']
</div>

The default value will change based on the current [lang](../text/lang).
