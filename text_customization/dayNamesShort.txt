
dayNamesShort *1.3*
===================

Abbreviated names of days-of-week.

<div class='spec' markdown='1'>
Array, *default*:

	['Sun', 'Mon', 'Tue', 'Wed', 'Thu', 'Fri', 'Sat']
</div>

<div class='version-info' markdown='1'>
Prior to version 1.3, this was possible by setting $.fullCalendar.dayAbbrevs
</div>
